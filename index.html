UJJAIN

Ujjain, located on the banks of the holy Kshipra River in Madhya Pradesh, is one of the most revered spiritual destinations in India. Known as the "City of Temples" and a prominent site in Hindu mythology, Ujjain is celebrated as one of the Sapta Puris—the seven sacred cities of India. It holds a special place in the hearts of devotees and travelers alike for its rich history, divine energy, and ancient architecture.

AYODHYA

Ayodhya, located in the northern state of Uttar Pradesh, is one of the most revered and ancient cities in India. Known as the "City of Lord Rama", Ayodhya holds immense religious significance for Hindus as the birthplace of Lord Rama, the seventh incarnation of Lord Vishnu. It is also one of the Sapta Puris, the seven holy cities that lead to salvation in Hinduism.

MATHURA

Mathura, located in Uttar Pradesh, is one of the holiest cities in India and an important pilgrimage site for Hindus. Known as the birthplace of Lord Krishna, the eighth incarnation of Lord Vishnu, Mathura is steeped in history, devotion, and culture. It is one of the Sapta Puris, the seven sacred cities that hold immense religious significance in Hinduism.

HARIDWAR

Haridwar, nestled in the state of Uttarakhand, is one of the holiest cities in India and a revered pilgrimage site for Hindus. Known as the "Gateway to the Gods", Haridwar is where the sacred River Ganga descends from the mountains to the plains. It is a place of immense spiritual significance and is counted among the Sapta Puris, the seven holy cities of Hinduism.

VARANASI

Varanasi, also known as Kashi or Banaras, is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world and a cornerstone of Indian spirituality. Located on the banks of the holy Ganga River in Uttar Pradesh, Varanasi is revered as the City of Light and is one of the Sapta Puris, the seven sacred cities in Hinduism. It is a melting pot of faith, culture, and tradition.

KANCHIPURAM

Kanchipuram, located in the state of Tamil Nadu, is one of the Sapta Puris, the seven sacred cities of Hinduism. Renowned as the "City of Thousand Temples", Kanchipuram is a center of spirituality, culture, and heritage. It is also celebrated for its exquisite silk sarees, which are an integral part of its identity.
